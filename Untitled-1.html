❓ Question: "If a TikTok starts with 100 views and triples every hour, how many views in 24 hours?"

✅ Calculation:
y = 100 · 3²⁴
3²⁴ = (3¹⁰)² · 3⁴ = (59,049)² · 81 ≈ 2.82 × 10¹¹
Multiply by 100 → ≈ 2.82 × 10¹³
